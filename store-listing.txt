Turn Google Drive into a real video review tool. This extension adds Frame.io-style timestamped comments to any video you watch in Google Drive — perfect for editors, agencies, marketing teams, course creators, and anyone reviewing video files shared over Drive.

WHAT IT DOES
============

• Auto-timestamp every comment. Click "Comment" while paused at any frame and the current video time is automatically inserted as [m:ss] at the start of your reply. No more typing timecodes by hand.

• Color-coded markers on the video timeline. Every timestamped comment drops a clickable circle on the seek bar, each in its own pastel color so you can tell them apart at a glance.

• Click a marker to jump and highlight. Click any timeline circle and the video seeks to that moment while the matching comment flashes in the same color — instant visual link between timeline and feedback.

• Click any [m:ss] inside a comment to jump. Every timestamp in every comment becomes a clickable blue link that seeks the video.

• Sort comments like a pro. Reorder Drive's comment list by Timecode, Oldest, Newest, Commenter, or filter to Completed (resolved) — the same sort modes Frame.io reviewers expect.

• One-click export to .txt. Download every comment with author, timestamp, and body as a clean text file for handoff, archiving, or pasting into your editing app.

WHY DRIVE INSTEAD OF FRAME.IO
=============================

You probably already keep your video files in Google Drive. You already share them with clients in Drive. Drive's comment system already exists — it just doesn't know what timestamps are. This extension adds the missing piece so you don't pay for, upload to, or sync with a separate review platform.

PRIVACY
=======

This extension runs entirely in your browser on drive.google.com pages only. It does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any data. It does not require an account, log-in, or external server. It does not read or modify videos themselves — only the comment text and timeline UI inside Drive.

HOW IT WORKS (UNDER THE HOOD)
=============================

The extension watches for Drive's video player and the comment editor. When you open a comment box, it reads the player's current time from the seek slider and inserts a [m:ss] timestamp. It scans existing comments for [m:ss] patterns and overlays clickable markers on the seek bar. Each timestamp is assigned a stable hue using golden-ratio distribution so colors stay distinct. Sort uses CSS layout overrides on Drive's comment list — non-destructive, framework-safe.

KEYBOARD-FRIENDLY
=================

Drive's existing keyboard shortcuts (k = play/pause, j/l = seek 10s, m = mute, f = fullscreen, c = captions) all keep working. Pause where you want, click Comment, type your note — the timestamp is already there.
